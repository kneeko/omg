presskit for oh my giraffe

oh my giraffe is a delightful game of survival.

Use your flexible neck to eat fruit while being chased by lions. Chew the fruits by the vine to send them plummeting onto the lions. Balance the two together to survive.

It was made by Nico Prins using the awesome, free and open-source game framework, LÖVE. It's available for iOS, Android, Windows, Mac OS X and Linux.

For additional information, contact nico@ohmygiraffe.com.
