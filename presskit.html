description

oh my giraffe is a delightful game of survival.

Eat fruits while being chased by lions. Snap the vines to send fruit plummeting onto the lions. Don't worry, your flexible neck will let you reach everything. You'll be great. And remember, the lions sleep at night. That's your chance to gobble down as many fruits as possible.

about

oh my giraffe was made by Nico Prins, a game developer based in Seattle. It's available on the App Store and Google Play, with a free preview for Windows, Mac OS X and Linux. It was built using the awesome, free and open-source game framework, LÖVE.
