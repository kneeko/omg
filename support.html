frequently asked questions

How do I escape the lions?

You have to snap the vines of some of the fruits. This will cause them to fall and hit the lions in the face.

How does the scoring works?

You get points for eating fruit, and as long you keep up eating the fruits you'll get more and more points for each one. Specifically, it's additive combo system (each fruit adds one point to your combo), and your combo stays alive as long as you eat a fruit every few seconds.

It runs really slowly and sometimes crashes, what can I do?

If you keep a lot of apps open at once, closing some of them or restarting your device can help. If you continue to have problems, please email us and we'll try our best to get you sorted.
